Getting Started

Investing in the stock market is one of the most effective ways to build wealth over time. Start by opening a brokerage account, determining your risk tolerance, and setting clear financial goals.

Types of Investments

Stocks: Ownership shares in individual companies. Higher risk but higher potential returns.

ETFs: Exchange-traded funds that track indexes, sectors, or themes. Provide instant diversification.

Bonds: Fixed-income investments that pay regular interest. Lower risk than stocks.

Building Your Portfolio

Diversification is key. Don't put all your money in one stock or sector. Consider a mix of domestic and international stocks, different sectors, and bonds based on your age and risk tolerance.
