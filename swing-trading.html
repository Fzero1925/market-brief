What is Swing Trading?

Swing trading involves holding positions for several days to weeks to capture price "swings" in the market. It's less intensive than day trading but more active than buy-and-hold investing.

Best Timeframes

Swing traders typically use daily and weekly charts to identify trends and entry/exit points. The 4-hour chart can also be useful for timing entries.
