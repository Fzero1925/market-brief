Calls, puts & strategies

What Are Options?

Options are contracts that give you the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell a stock at a specific price before a certain date.

Call Options

A call option gives you the right to BUY a stock at a specific price (strike price) before expiration. Buy calls when you expect the stock price to rise.

Put Options

A put option gives you the right to SELL a stock at a specific price before expiration. Buy puts when you expect the stock price to fall, or to hedge existing positions.

Basic Strategies

Covered Call: Own the stock and sell call options to generate income.

Protective Put: Own the stock and buy put options as insurance.

Cash-Secured Put: Sell put options with cash ready to buy the stock if assigned.
